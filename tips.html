Health and Fitness

Advantages of Fitness

  • Strong body and muscles
  • Boosts energy level for daily work
  • Improves immunity to fight diseases
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves mental health and confidence
  • Helps maintain ideal body weight

Healthy Food List

  • Fruits - rich in vitamins and fiber
  • Green vegetables - good for digestion
  • Milk and Eggs - high protein foods
  • Nuts - healthy fats for brain
  • Whole grains - provide long energy
  • Fresh juices - keep body hydrated

Fitness Activities

  • Running - improves heart health
  • Walking - best simple daily exercise
  • Yoga - improves flexibility and peace of mind
  • Cycling - strengthens legs
  • Gym workout - builds muscle strength
  • Dance - fun way to burn calories

Healthy Habits

  • Drink 7-8 glasses of water daily
  • Proper sleep of at least 7 hours
  • Avoid junk food and sugary drinks
  • Daily exercise for 30 minutes
  • Positive thinking and meditation
  • Maintain personal hygiene
